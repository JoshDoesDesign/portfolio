Angry Bovine is a visual design agency based out of Boulder, Colorado. During my time at Angry Bovine I reported directly to the Creative Director and owner, which helped me to develop proficiency in web and digital design. Clients included: Adobe Systems, Automation Anywhere, Chrono Track, Newco, Sovrn, VictorOps and WW Reynolds.

When it came to creating high fidelity visual mockups of websites, the tool of choice at Angry Bovine was Adobe Photoshop. Branding assignments and wireframing required Adobe Illustrator and print design assignments required Adobe Indesign. In short, my experience at Angry Bovine helped me to become proficient in the Adobe Creative Suite.
