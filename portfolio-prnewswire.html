PR Newswire is a news agency that offers news distribution, targeting, monitoring and marketing solutions to help clients connect and engage with target audiences across the globe. As an Implementer with PR Newswire, I am specifically working for a division of PR Newswire that focuses on creating microsites and content management systems (CMS) for clients. My main responsibilities consist of interface design and front end development of client websites.

One of the sites I helped to redesign was the mediaroom site for Novo Nordisk. As the lead designer on the project, I created a variety of high fidelity design mockups of an existing site in order to redesign the interface. After a series of revisions based on client requests, a final design was approved and our team began to implement the designs and push the new site live.
