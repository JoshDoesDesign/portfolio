Even though I specialize in web design and development, I have worked on other visual design projects in the past. Having a strong eye for design makes you a better developer, as a design centered approach makes you more empathetic to the customer's needs.

Colorado is home to some of the most beautiful mountains in the world. 53 of the mountains in the state are over 14,000 feel tall, thus they are known as the Colorado 14ers. I decided to create a logo for each of these massive peaks - each logo tells a story to the history of the mountain. If you are interested in viewing all 53 logos I made, CLICK HERE.

Strange Music, Inc. is an American independent record label based out of Kansas City, Missouri. I decided to redesign the visual layout of the company's homepage as a personal project. One gload of the redesign was to restructure the original page's content in a more organized manner. The newly redesigned layout features large 'call to action' marquees, along with appropriately titled and sectioned content. By improving the site's organizational structure a visitor will have an easier time engaging with the interface. CLICK HERE to view the interface redesign.
