Trimble is a company that mixes hardware, software and mobility technologies in order to streamline communication and collaboration throughout a construction company's lifecycle. As a Trimble contractor I created an interactive design mockup of the Inspection Manager web application interface for Trimble's Heavy Civil Construction unit. The typical user of the Inspection Manager would be a work site inspector, whose primary responsibility is to make sure equipment on the job site is up to par for the construction team to use.

The Inspection Manager app allows the work site inspector to filter equipment based on the condition of an item: 'Pass', 'Action' or 'Fail'. The app also gives the user access to a map view of the inspection locations, as well as the option to generate Inspection Reports for each inspected item on a job site.
